# Introduction

Altrum AI combines **Advanced AI Guardrails** with the **AI Gateway** to give you **centralised control, automated compliance, and real-time risk management,** for every AI system in your enterprise.

## At a Glance

| **Feature** | **What It Means for You** |
| --- | --- |
| **Advanced AI Guardrails** | Detect and block risks like hallucinations, bias, data leaks, and prompt injections, in real-time |
| **AI Gateway** | High-performance, vendor-agnostic integration layer to connect and govern all AI systems in one place. |
| **No-Code Guardrails Management** | Empower non-technical teams to set and enforce guardrails in minutes. |
| **Real-Time Risk Monitoring** | Track every prompt and response with automated alerts and mitigation. |
| **Enterprise-Wide Visibility** | One dashboard for all AI activity, compliance status, and performance insights. |

## Why Altrum AI?

- **Freedom to Innovate** — Accelerate AI adoption without increasing risk.
- **Full Regulatory Confidence** — Stay aligned with EU AI Act, GDPR, HIPAA, FCA and emerging laws.
- **Scalable Governance** — Apply controls across every AI app, model, and department.
- **Faster Time-to-Value** — Deploy in minutes, integrate without disrupting existing workflows.
