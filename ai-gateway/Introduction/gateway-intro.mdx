## Overview

The AltrumAI Enterprise AI Gateway is a unified, secure, and scalable API abstraction layer that seamlessly integrates with the AltrumAI governance platform to provide organisations with controlled, compliant, and efficient access to multiple AI providers. It serves as the critical bridge between your AI guardrails, policies & controls defined in AltrumAI and the actual AI applications your teams build, ensuring every AI interaction adheres to your organisation's standards, security requirements, and regulatory compliance needs.

## Why Enterprise AI Gateway?

### For Business Leaders

**Strategic Control & Risk Management**
- **AI Governance at Scale**: Enforce company-wide AI policies automatically without slowing innovation
- **Cost Optimisation**: Monitor and control AI spend across all departments and AI Systems
- **Vendor Independence**: Avoid vendor lock in to any single AI provider, maintaining negotiation leverage
- **Compliance Assurance**: Meet regulatory requirements (EU's AI Act, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2) for your AI Products with built-in controls
- **Risk Mitigation**: Prevent data leaks, inappropriate content, and harmful AI outputs

**Business Agility**
- Switch AI providers instantly based on cost, performance, or capabilities
- Test new AI models without changing application code
- Scale AI adoption safely across the organisation
- Maintain audit trails for all AI interactions across your organisations AI systems

### For Developers

**Developer Productivity**
- **Single API Interface**: Use one OpenAI compatible API for all AI providers
- **No Code Changes**: Switch between GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and other providers without changing application code
- **SDK Compatibility**: Works with existing OpenAI SDKs and integrations in Python, JavaScript, and other languages
- **Streaming Support**: Supports streaming responses for better user experience in your AI Products
- **Built-in Resilience**: Automatic error handling and failover capabilities

**Operational Excellence**
- Centralised monitoring and logging
- Performance optimisation with intelligent caching
- Rate limiting and quota management
- Comprehensive error handling and debugging

### For Compliance & Security Teams

**Enterprise-grade Security and Compliance**
- **Data Privacy**: No storage of sensitive request/response data unless explicitly enabled by the user
- **Secret Management**: Secure handling of Provider API keys and credentials
- **Access Controls**: Role-based permissions integrated with AltrumAI platform
- **Audit Logging**: Complete trail of all AI interactions across your AI systems
- **Sensitive Data Protection**: Automatic detection and redaction of sensitive information & PII from your AI interactions
