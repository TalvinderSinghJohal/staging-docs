## Navigating the Home Page

Once logged in, you'll be directed to the **Dashboard**, the control center of your AltrumAI experience.

### The Home Page Includes:
- **Navigation Panel** – Quick access to Dashboard, Administration, Reporting, Observability, Support, Documentation.
- **Summary Statistics** – See key stats, charts and graphs on projects, requests, and violations
- **Project List** – Table view of all projects with management tools

## Create and Configure a New Project

### Launch the Project Creation Flow

- Click the **Create Project** button in the navigation panel  
  _or_  
  Click the small blue **+ icon** in the bottom-right corner of the screen
- You will be redirected to the **Create Project** page

### Enter Project Details

- Fill in the following fields:
  - **Project Name** (required)
  - **Project Description** (required)
  - **Project Tags** (optional)
  - **Project Owner** (required)
  - **LLM Service Providers**
- Click **Create & Continue**

### Configure the Project

After creating the project, you’ll be taken to the project setup environment which includes 3 key tabs:

#### Configure Policies  
Define the policies for your project. Select from the list of AltrumAI-provided policies. Once a policy is selected:
- Configuration options will appear on the right-hand side
- Use the toggle to enable the policy
- Configure enforcement modes, thresholds, or entities depending on the policy type
> **Note:**
> To learn more, refer to the [Policy Configuration Guide](/product-guide/policies).

#### Configure Response  
Customise how the AI responds within the context of your project.

#### Test Policies  
Use this sandbox chat environment to test configured policies in real time and validate outcomes.

### Continue to Integration

- On the **Test Policies** tab, click **Continue to Integration** to move to the integration step

### Configure Integration

- Choose an **Integration Type** from the available options
- Your **API key** will be displayed after selection
- Click **Continue to Summary**

### Review and Publish

- On the **Project Summary** page:
  - Review your project settings and configuration
  - Edit previous steps if needed
- Click **Publish Project**  
  A success message will confirm your project has been published
- Click **View Project** to go directly to the Project Dashboard

---

## Project Dashboard

After publishing, the **Project Dashboard** displays all key details and project metrics in a central location.

---

## Editing Project Details

1. Navigate to the **Dashboard** and locate your project in the **Project Table**
2. In the **Actions** column, click the **ellipsis** icon
3. Click **View Project** to open the Project Dashboard
4. In the first section titled **Project Details**, click the **Edit** button
5. You will be redirected to **Step 1** of the project setup
6. Make necessary changes and click **Save Changes**

---

## Best Practices

- Always test policy configurations in the **Test Policies** tab before publishing
- Assign appropriate **Project Owners** to maintain oversight
- Use descriptive descriptions and relevant tags to improve project information capture
