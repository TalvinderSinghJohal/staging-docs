## Standard Login URL

- Visit: [https://platform.altrum.ai](https://platform.altrum.ai)
- If you are onboarded via the organisation signup process, you can access the platform directly using your credentials.

## Invitation-Based Access

- Users will receive a **signup email** with a **unique access link**.
- If you don't receive it:
  - Contact your **organisation platform admin**
  - Or email **support@altrum.ai**

## Logging In to AltrumAI

1. Visit [https://platform.altrum.ai](https://platform.altrum.ai)
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Login**

![Placeholder for login screenshot]

## Resetting Your Password

1. On the login page, click **Forgot your Password?**
2. Enter your **registered email address** and click **Send**
3. Check your inbox for a **password reset link**
4. Follow the instructions to create a **new secure password**

![Placeholder for forgot password screenshot]

## Inviting New Users (Admin Only)

1. Navigate to **User Management** from the Navigation Panel
2. Click **Invite User**
3. Enter the user’s email and assign a role (Admin/User)
4. Click **Invite**

![Placeholder for invite user screenshot]

## Signup Process (Invited Users)

1. Click the signup link in the email
2. Complete all **required fields** and click **Submit**
3. Confirm your account via the **verification email**
4. You’ll be redirected to the login page to access the platform

![Placeholder for signup process screenshot]
