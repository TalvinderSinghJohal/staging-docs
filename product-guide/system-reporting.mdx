## Overview

The **AI System Reporting** module provides real-time insights into the performance of your AI systems and the effectiveness of guardrail enforcement.

AI System Reporting enables you to track key usage statistics, monitor guardrail adherence, and identify trends over time empowering better governance and continuous improvement.

<img height="200" src="/images/reporting/ai-system-report.png" />

---

## Accessing the AI System Reporting Dashboard

1. From the **Navigation Panel**, click on the **Reporting** tab and select **AI Systems**
2. You will be directed to the **AI System Reporting Page**, where you can access detailed insights for your AI systems.

---

## Navigating the AI System Reporting Page

The AI System Reporting Page provides comprehensive metrics and analytics for your AI systems:

### Key Metrics

- **Usage Statistics**: Track request volume, response times, and model performance
- **Guardrail Violations**: Monitor compliance and identify potential issues
- **Performance Trends**: Analyze patterns over time to optimize system performance

### Time Filter Options

- **Last 7 Days**
- **Last 30 Days** 
- **Last 90 Days**
- **Year to Date**

Selecting a timeframe will automatically update the statistics and visualisations.

---

## Best Practices

- **Regular Monitoring**: Check the AI System Reporting Dashboard regularly to ensure continuous compliance
- **Trend Analysis**: Use time filters to identify patterns and spot early warning signs
- **Performance Optimization**: Monitor usage patterns to optimize costs and performance
