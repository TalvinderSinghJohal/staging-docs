## Overview

The **Reporting** feature gives you real-time visibility into how your AI applications are performing and how well they are adhering to configured policies.

## Accessing Reports

1. Navigate to the **Reporting** tab in the navigation panel
2. The dashboard will display default metrics for your active projects

![Placeholder for reporting tab screenshot]

## Filtering Report Data

1. Choose a **time range** (e.g., Last 7 Days, Last 30 Days)
2. Apply **filters** such as project name, violation type, or user
3. The dashboard will automatically update based on your selections

![Placeholder for filtering dashboard screenshot]

## Available Metrics

- **AI Model Usage** – Volume of prompts processed and responses generated
- **Policy Violations** – Counts and categories of ethical policy breaches
- **Violation Trends** – Time-series view of how violations are changing over time

![Placeholder for metrics dashboard screenshot]

## Exporting Reports (Coming Soon)

Exporting dashboard data to CSV or PDF formats will be available in an upcoming release.

## Best Practices

- Review dashboards weekly to stay informed about compliance
- Use filters to investigate spikes or anomalies in violation activity
