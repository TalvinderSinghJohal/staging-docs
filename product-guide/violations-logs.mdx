# Observability

Gain full visibility into your AI Gateway and guardrail activity. The Observability dashboard provides real-time logs, metrics, and analytics to help you monitor usage, detect anomalies, and ensure compliance.

## Key Features

- **Request & Response Logs:**  
  View detailed logs of all API requests and responses passing through your AI Gateway, including timestamps, status codes, latency, and provider details.

- **Guardrail Events:**  
  Track when guardrails are triggered, including blocked, flagged, or modified requests, and see which policies were applied.

- **Usage Metrics:**  
  Analyze usage patterns over timeâ€”see request volumes, error rates, and guardrail activations by project, user, or provider.

- **Alerts & Notifications:**  
  Set up alerts for unusual activity, such as spikes in errors or frequent guardrail triggers.

- **Export & Audit:**  
  Export logs for compliance audits or further analysis.

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the **Observability** tab in the platform sidebar.
2. Filter logs and metrics by date, project, user, or provider.
3. Click on individual log entries for detailed request/response information.
4. Review guardrail events to understand how your policies are being enforced.
5. Set up custom alerts to stay informed about critical events.

> **Tip:**  
> Use Observability to proactively monitor your AI systems, quickly troubleshoot issues, and demonstrate compliance with organizational or regulatory requirements.
